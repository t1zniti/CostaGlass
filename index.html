About Costa Glass

Glass Enclosures in Marbella

We are experts in glass enclosures. If you're looking for the perfect solution to enhance your spaces — terraces, penthouses, patios, local businesses, and more — you've come to the right place.

At CostaGlass, we have a highly skilled multidisciplinary team that takes care of the entire process of installing your glass enclosures.

We are manufacturers and installers with years of experience providing our services across the entire Costa del Sol. We offer glass curtain enclosures fully customized to your needs. At CostaGlass, every design is made to measure. In addition, we provide tempered glass options — clear, frosted, or with decorative finishes — to match the style of your space. Our team will guide you to ensure a seamless integration with your surroundings.
