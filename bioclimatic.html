Compact System

110° Axial Opening Technology

The Costa Glass Compact System is an innovative shading solution that offers a 110° axial opening via a special motor mechanism integrated into the roof and vertical legs in mobile aluminum roof systems. This design allows you to adjust the sun's rays according to your preferences, providing flexibility and control over lighting.

The system opens partially using advanced rotation technology, offering solid insulation and durability thanks to water sealing with wicking and gaskets.

In addition to its efficient water evacuation, the interior chambers of the panels provide resistance to wind force. This system provides total insulation against inclement weather, both hot and cold, while interior lighting is achieved through LEDs integrated into the profiles.

Innovative Design

Pioneering Gutter Joining System

Costa Glass presents an innovative gutter joining system specially designed for multiple modules installed contiguously. This unique design allows for efficient water discharge between the modules, and a single strut profile can support two systems simultaneously.

This pioneering system offers a 15% increase in water collection/drainage compared to similar systems, providing exceptional performance in terms of efficiency and space utilization. The presence of more openings contributes to creating a more spacious environment, ideal for enjoying open and bright surroundings.

With a width between two rails of 4500 mm and a maximum expansion of 7500 mm, our systems offer unmatched versatility, adapting to diverse needs and providing efficient solutions to transform your spaces.
