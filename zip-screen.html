Zip Screen

Protection and Comfort for Your Outdoor Spaces

Exterior Shading

Innovative Retractable Shading

Costa Glass zip screens are innovative exterior shading solutions designed to enhance outdoor spaces, offering protection against sun, wind, rain, and insects. These systems consist of retractable screens that, when deployed, create enclosed areas providing privacy and comfort without obstructing views.

Benefits

Why Choose Zip Screens

Wind Resistance

Designed to withstand various weather conditions, Costa Glass zip screens offer durability and reliability, resisting winds up to 150 km/h.

Insect Protection

The fine mesh of Costa Glass zip screens acts as an effective barrier against insects, allowing you to enjoy outdoor spaces without annoyance.

Privacy and Comfort

By enclosing outdoor areas, Costa Glass zip screens provide greater privacy and help regulate temperature, creating comfortable environments throughout the year.

Aesthetic Integration

Available in a wide range of colors and designs, Costa Glass zip screens can be customized to complement your home's aesthetic, integrating perfectly with the architectural style.

Residential Use

Perfect for Your Home

Ideal for patios, balconies, pergolas, and terraces, Costa Glass zip screens expand your living space and protect it from inclement weather.

Commercial Use

Enhance Your Business

Restaurants, cafes, and hotel establishments use Costa Glass zip screens to create comfortable outdoor dining areas, improving the customer experience.

Customization

Configure Your Perfect System

Size & Coverage Determine the dimensions of the area you wish to cover to ensure the zip screen system fits properly.
Fabric Options Choose from various mesh densities and materials for the desired level of privacy, light filtering, and insect protection.
Operating Mechanism Choose between manual or motorized systems, with motorized ones offering remote control and smart home integration.
