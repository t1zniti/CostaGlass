Specialists in Glass Curtains

We specialize in frameless space enclosures called Glass Curtains. Service quality is one of the most important parameters for our glazing company, enabling us to achieve maximum utilization of both open spaces (balconies, terraces, porches, etc.) and divided spaces (offices, private offices, meeting rooms, etc.).

Imagine being able to enjoy your terrace all year round, regardless of the season or weather. This is possible thanks to our glass curtain systems.

We are one of the few companies that not only installs glass curtains in Malaga but also develops them, manufacturing our own glass curtain system. This allows us to innovate and, above all, save costs.
