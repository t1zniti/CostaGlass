Glass Windscreens

Invisible Protection, Maximum Elegance

Paravientos de Cristal

A Barrier That Disappears Into Your Landscape

Glass windscreens are the ultimate solution for protecting your outdoor terrace, patio, or pool area from wind, rain, and noise — without sacrificing your view or the open feel of your space. Crafted from architectural tempered glass and precision-machined aluminium hardware, they provide a robust barrier that is virtually invisible.

Costa Glass glass windscreens can be installed as standalone panels or in interconnected sets, allowing you to enclose any space with a clean, frameless aesthetic. Whether you need to shelter a restaurant terrace, a private villa pool deck, or a rooftop lounge, our windscreens deliver year-round comfort and protection.

Each system is made to measure and available in a curated selection of hardware finishes and glass options, ensuring a seamless integration with any architectural style — from contemporary to classic.

Mounting Configurations

Adapts to Any Space

Our glass windscreen systems are designed to adapt to virtually any architectural layout. Four mounting configurations are available, giving you the flexibility to achieve the perfect enclosure for your specific project.

  • Freestanding — Base-plate mounted directly into the ground. No walls required, ideal for open terraces and pool areas.
  • Wall-Fixed — Panels secured to an existing wall with concealed brackets for clean lines and structural support.
  • Hybrid — Combines wall mounting and freestanding posts, perfect for L-shaped or U-shaped terrace enclosures.
  • Recessed Channel — Glass sits within a flush floor channel. The most minimalist option — no visible hardware at all.
Finishes & Customisation

Fully Tailored to Your Project

Every component of a Costa Glass windscreen — from the aluminium clamps and rails to the base channels — is available in a choice of premium hardware finishes. Custom RAL colours are available on request to match any design scheme.

Glass is available in multiple options to suit privacy, solar control, or purely aesthetic preferences: Clear, Green Tint, Grey Tint, Blue Tint, and Frosted. All panels use architectural-grade tempered or laminated safety glass, complying with rigorous European safety standards and offering outstanding wind resistance.

Optional upgrades include integrated LED strip lighting, rain and wind sensors, home automation compatibility, solar-tinted glass, smart frosted privacy film, acoustic lamination, and self-cleaning nano-coating — allowing your windscreen to perform as intelligently as it looks.
